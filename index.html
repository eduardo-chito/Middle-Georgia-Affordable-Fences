Fence Services

We focus on fence installation and repairs for homeowners and small businesses in Middle Georgia.

Wood Privacy Fences

Classic 6' and 8' privacy fences built with quality posts and pickets. Dog-ear, picture-frame, and board-on-board styles available to match your home and yard.

Chain Link Fences

Durable and cost-effective fencing for pets, side yards, and commercial areas. Galvanized or black vinyl-coated chain link with walk-through and drive gates.

Vinyl Fences

Low-maintenance and long-lasting vinyl fences available in white, tan, and other modern styles. Great for clean appearance, privacy, and durability with no painting required.

Gates & Fence Repairs

Custom single and double-drive gates, plus repairs for leaning posts, warped pickets, and storm damage to keep your existing fence safe and functional.

Fence Painting

Professional fence painting and staining to protect your wood fence from weather damage and bring new life to old or faded panels.

About Middle Georgia Affordable Fences

Middle Georgia Affordable Fences LLC is a locally owned, family-operated fence company serving homeowners and small businesses across Middle Georgia. We are owned and operated by myself (Eduardo Osuna) and my stepdad (Oscar Hernandez). We believe a fence should be straight, sturdy, and built to last — not something you have to worry about repairing a year later.

From the first measurement to the final walkthrough, we focus on clear communication, clean work, and respectful treatment of your property. Our goal is simple: install a fence you’re proud to show off.
